Who am I

I was born on 1990 on a humble family around Madrid. When I was a kid I cannot stop drawing and writting stories. Also, I have a huge library that my mother says I read when I was at school.

I wanted to study story. I really love to read about Romans, Greeks and learning their culture, traditions and legends. But 6 months after I started the college, I realized that was not for me.

I was more creative than that. I couldn't just memorize dates and facts. So I quit. My mother was really sad and frustraded, as I was. But something happened. That's why started to believe that karma exists.

A wonderful person that I was living with convinced me to study Software Engineering. I couldn't believe that was for me. Even if I loved computers, and do things with them. But somehow, I went to pick more information and I was amazed. I immediatly felt at home. So I decided to study there.

It wasn't easy. I had no idea about mathematics, physics and that stuff. I spent 6 months preparing by myself for that, before the beginning of the degree. But somehow, four years and a half later, I became a Software Engineer.

And the rest is history.

Apart from that, I didn't stopped with my passions. I kept writting. And I started a illustration degree, but was so tough to do while I was working, so I stopped It. Past year I started a design one, but I had a lot of personal and professional problems and I quit.

I don't think that those failures were something bad. I grew a lot and learn a lot from myself while doing them. Now I'm interested on online courses and Design meetups. I'm not going to stop at all. Just keep trying until I find the right formula for me.
